About

Myself

Electrical Engineering Co-op Student

My name is Vincent Yee and I am a student at the University of Alberta currently studying in an Electrical Engineering Co-op program. I believe persistence is what defines me most. It was a trait that my parents always emphasized. I never had to be the best. I never had to know how to do everything. But regardless of the situation as long as I worked my hardest, put my best foot forward, and never give up in spite of all the difficulty, it would be enough for them. My parents taught me to pursue passion instead of paychecks and to stand for quality instead of quantity. To work hard in silence and celebrate my successes humbly. I believe smarts, talent, and innate gifts are not the formula to success, but instead achieved from hard work, perseverance, and sacrifice. The development of strong relationships is the foundation of everything I do, both professionally and personally. I treat others with kindness and treasure my friends and family like they're my all. I try to live in such a way where if someone spoke badly of me, no one would believe it. As the saying goes: 'Don't do unto others what you don't want done unto you'.
